About Us

BioNanoMedIt take this opportunity to announce "3rd International Congress & Expo on Biotechnology and Bioengineering (ICEBB-2018)" during June 25-26, 2018 at Jessore, Bangladesh, Malaysia which will bring together world leaders in their respective fields in the fascinating environment of Jessore, Bangladesh.
ICEBB-2018 prepared a platform to have open discussion with your pal's who has a similar thought like you. BioNanoMedItwarm welcome to world leader's and professional experts to participate in the International Congress on Biotechnology and Bioengineering to share your valuable innovations and suggestions to the next generations.
BioNanoMedItaims to bring together front-line experts from multidisciplinary research and application areas to join this conference, to discuss the benefits of Biotechnology and Bioengineering in their Research and Development efforts to advance the networking, and collaborating between different academia, research and market leaders in the field and to stimulate the exchange of educational concepts.
ICEBB-2018 will introduce the delegates to the new developments and breakthroughs in many disciplines of Biotechnology and Bioengineering through various speakers and workshop sessions. It will feature a scientific program of Keynote lectures and invited talks by world-eminent personalities, special sessions, debates, scientific discussions, oral and poster presentations of peer-reviewed contributions to share the most recently breaking research development, discovery and industrial progress.On behalf of ICEBB-2018, BioNanoMedItinvites you to take this opportunity to join us for scientific extravaganza.
